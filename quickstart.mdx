# Quickstart

This guide will walk you through setting up PlanningSup and viewing your first calendar.

## Access PlanningSup

You can use PlanningSup in three ways:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web app">
    Visit [planningsup.app](https://planningsup.app) in your browser. No installation required.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Browser extension">
    Install the [Chrome extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/planningsup/bcjhdlcgblcphljokeippholkfifdbod) for quick access from your toolbar.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PWA (recommended)">
    On mobile or desktop, tap the install prompt or use your browser's "Install app" option for an offline-capable native experience.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Select your planning

When you first open PlanningSup, you'll see the welcome screen:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the planning picker">
    Click **"Choisir mon planning"** (Choose my planning) or press the `U` key
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for your university">
    Use the search bar to filter by university name, department, or program
    
    ```
    Example: "INSA Rennes" or "IUT Vannes"
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the planning tree">
    Expand groups to find your specific year and section
    
    <Tip>Most universities organize plannings as: **University > Department > Year > Group**</Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select one or more plannings">
    Click the checkbox next to your planning. You can select multiple plannings to merge them into a single calendar view.
    
    <Info>Multi-planning is useful for students with options across multiple groups</Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close the picker">
    Click outside the dialog or press `Esc`. Your calendar will load immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate your calendar

Once your planning is loaded, you can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="arrow-left">
    **Previous period**
    
    Press `←` or click the left arrow in the calendar controls
  </Card>
  <Card icon="arrow-right">
    **Next period**
    
    Press `→` or click the right arrow in the calendar controls
  </Card>
  <Card icon="calendar-day">
    **Change view**
    
    Switch between Day / Week / Month Grid / Month Agenda views
  </Card>
  <Card icon="calendar-check">
    **Go to today**
    
    Click "Aujourd'hui" (Today) to jump to the current date
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The calendar automatically refreshes:
  - On page load
  - When the window regains focus
  - Every 5 minutes in the background
</Note>

## View event details

Click any event in your calendar to see:

- Event title and type (lecture, lab, tutorial)
- Start and end times
- Location (room or building)
- Teacher name (if available)
- Additional notes

## Optional: Sign in for sync

By default, your planning selection and preferences are stored **locally** in your browser. To sync them across devices:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the user menu">
    Click your avatar or the login button in the top-right corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a provider">
    Sign in with Discord or GitHub
    
    <Tip>You can also use **passkeys** (WebAuthn) for passwordless authentication on the web version</Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the app">
    Grant PlanningSup access to your account (we only read your profile)
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once signed in, your settings will sync automatically:

- Selected plannings
- Custom colors
- Event blocklist
- Custom planning groups
- UI preferences (show weekends, merge duplicates, etc.)

<Warning>
  On mobile or extension platforms, passkeys are not supported. Use social login instead.
</Warning>
